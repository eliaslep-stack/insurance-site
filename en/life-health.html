Life & health insurance: what matters before you commit

The right life or health policy is not the one with the best marketing. It’s the one that fits your profile: age, smoking status, the level of protection you want, and how you expect to use private healthcare (hospitalization, diagnostics, outpatient benefits).

Most problems happen when people choose based only on the premium. What really matters is the structure: deductibles, limits, exclusions, pre-existing condition rules, and how claims are handled in practice. That’s why we ask only for the essentials, so we can propose options that make sense—rather than a “blind price”.

If your goal is family or loan protection, the life insurance sum is central. If your goal is private medical access, then the right hospital/outpatient structure is what makes the difference.
