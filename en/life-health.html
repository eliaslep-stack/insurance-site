Hospital Cover

Plans that cover private hospitals in Greece and abroad, with clear deductibles and transparent limits. Together we choose between open network and closed network solutions, depending on your budget and the hospitals you prefer.

Capitation & Outpatient Care

Capitation programs give you access to a specific hospital group with a fixed annual cost and predictable participation. Outpatient covers diagnostics, visits and exams so that you do not postpone preventive checks for financial reasons.

Life Insurance & Income Protection

Life insurance protects your family from the financial impact of a sudden loss. We can combine pure term cover with savings or unit linked, depending on whether your priority is protection, savings or both.

How We Work Together

We start from your real needs and your budget, compare programs from different companies and explain the exclusions one by one. You receive written proposals with scenarios and you decide without pressure, knowing exactly what is covered and what is not.
