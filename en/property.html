Property Insurance

Cover for your home or business premises against fire, earthquake, theft and everyday risks.

Home Insurance

We insure apartments and houses for owners and tenants. The cover can include building, contents or both and can be adapted to mortgage requirements or independent personal needs.

Business Premises

Offices, shops, warehouses and professional spaces need different limits and clauses than a simple home plan. We look at lease contracts, landlord obligations and any bank requirements before we suggest a solution.

Earthquake and Natural Hazards

Earthquake remains one of the most important risks in Greece. We review insured values, construction year and structure type so that the capital insured reflects the real reconstruction cost and not just the market value.

The Process

You send us basic information about the property, we run comparisons with several insurers and send you clear written options. You see in detail what is included, what is optional and how each extra cover affects the premium.
