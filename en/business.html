Understanding Liability Insurance

Liability insurance covers financial claims arising from bodily injury or property damage caused to third parties. The real value of a policy lies in its limits, exclusions and wording — not only in the premium.

Choosing limits that are too low can expose you to serious personal or business risk, while excessive limits may lead to unnecessary cost. A proper assessment balances exposure, activity and real-world scenarios.
