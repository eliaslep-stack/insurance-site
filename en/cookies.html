Cookies Policy

We use cookies only for essential site functions and basic analytics.

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They are used to remember preferences and technical information.

Which cookies we use

We use technical cookies to ensure the proper functioning of the site (such as language selection) and possibly anonymised analytics cookies to understand site traffic. We do not use cookies for targeted advertising.

How you can control cookies

You can configure your browser to block or delete cookies. However, disabling technical cookies may affect the correct operation of the site.
