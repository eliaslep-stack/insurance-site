Car insurance in Greece: what to consider before you choose

Car insurance in Greece is mandatory, but choosing the right policy should not be based on price alone. Each vehicle, driver profile, and usage pattern comes with different risks and needs.

The mandatory third-party liability cover protects against damage you cause to others, but it does not cover your own vehicle. That’s why many drivers add options such as roadside assistance, glass cover, theft, fire, natural hazards, legal protection or comprehensive coverage.

The cost of car insurance is influenced by factors like engine size, first registration year, location, claims history and how the vehicle is used. In practice, there is no “one-size-fits-all” policy.
