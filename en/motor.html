Motor Insurance

From mandatory third party cover to full comprehensive plans with roadside assistance and glass cover.

Third Party and Extended Covers

We start from the legal minimum and add fire, theft, natural perils and other extensions only where they make sense for the value and use of the vehicle.

Comprehensive and Driver Cover

Comprehensive cover protects your own car after an at fault accident. Driver personal accident covers you as a person, not only the vehicle, and is particularly important in Greece where many drivers are under-insured.

Roadside Assistance and Glass Cover

Roadside assistance and towing, as well as glass cover, are often low cost compared to the benefit they offer. We check whether they are included in the base plan or need to be added separately.

Claims Support

In case of an accident you contact me directly or the assistance line of the insurer. I help you collect documents, communicate with the repair shop and follow up the claim until payment or completion of repairs.
